"Deforestation" is a website that uses a deep learning model to detect and monitor illegal activities in the Amazon rainforest. The platform provides a user-friendly interface for environmental conservation NGOs in South America to access and analyze data about deforestation in the region. NGOs can use the platform to collaborate, visualize the impact of deforestation over time, and take action to protect the forest and its inhabitants.

Created by using mainly ruby on rails, html, scss and sql and Javascript. Tranlated with i18n.

Headhunter is a paid subscription website for recruiters to find and hire talented professionals. Recruiters can post job openings, search for candidates, and manage the hiring process through a user-friendly interface. In addition, the platform has an automatic matching and suggesting system that recommends the best candidates for a given job based on their skills, experience, and qualifications. This system helps recruiters save time and effort by providing them with a list of potential candidates who are likely to be a good fit for the job. The platform also provides advanced search filters to further refine the candidate pool and ensure that recruiters find the best match for their job openings.

Created by using mainly ruby on rails, html, scss and sql and Javascript.

"Go Green" is an environment-friendly app that helps individuals reduce their carbon footprint in various aspects of their lives. The app covers areas such as groceries, clothing, education, activities, jobs, and volunteering. Users can access information, tips, and resources to make eco-friendly choices in these areas and track their progress towards a greener lifestyle. The app encourages users to make small changes that can have a big impact on the environment and provides a platform for like-minded individuals to connect and share ideas.

At the moment this project is still in progess but so far created by using Ruby on Rails, html, sass, sql, Javascript.
